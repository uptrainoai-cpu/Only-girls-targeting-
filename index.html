‚ùì Team FAQ (Quick Answers)

Q1: Ads par boys kyun aa rahe hain?

Aapne shayad Advantage+ Audience ON rakha hai. Isse OFF karein aur "Narrow Audience" mein specific brands (e.g. Nykaa/FabIndia) dalein jo ladke use nahi karte.

Q2: Leads bahut mehngi (Expensive) aa rahi hain?

Agar aap sirf Tier 1 cities target kar rahe hain toh leads mehngi hongi. Tier 2 cities (Lucknow, Jaipur) mix karein. Also, creative change karke dekhein.

Q3: Housewife ke liye best time kya hai?

Dophar 2 PM se 5 PM best time hai jab wo free hoti hain.

Q4: Muslim targeting ke liye best keyword?

"Modest Fashion" aur "Abaya" sabse safe aur accurate keywords hain.
