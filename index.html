‚ùì Team FAQ (Complete Solutions)

Har possible question ka detailed answer - Nadeem & Salma ke liye

Q1: Ads par boys kyun aa rahe hain?

Solution:
1) Advantage+ Audience OFF karein
2) "Narrow Audience" mein specific brands dalein (Nykaa, FabIndia)
3) Gender = Female explicitly set karein
4) Beauty/Fashion interests add karein

Q2: Leads bahut mehngi (Expensive) aa rahi hain?

Solution:
1) Tier 2 cities mix karein (Lucknow, Jaipur)
2) Age bracket expand karein (18-45)
3) Lookalike audiences test karein
4) Creative change karke dekhein
5) Behavioral targeting add karein

Q3: Housewife ke liye best time kya hai?

Answer: Dophar 2-5 PM best time hai jab wo free hoti hain
Alternative: Raat 8-11 PM (family time ke baad)
Avoid: Subah 9-12 (kaam ka time)

Q4: Muslim targeting ke liye best keyword?

Safe Keywords: "Modest Fashion", "Abaya", "Hijab"
Festival: "Ramadan", "Eid Shopping", "Halal"
Avoid: Religious keywords (algorithm sensitive)

Q5: Tier 1 vs Tier 2 mein kya difference hai?

Tier 1: ‚Çπ150-400 per lead, Premium products, English+Hindi
Tier 2: ‚Çπ50-150 per lead, Mid-range products, Hindi main
Strategy: Dono ko alag campaigns mein rakhein

Q6: Jewelry targeting kaise karein?

Premium: Tanishq, CaratLane followers + Tier 1 + Age 25-40
Fashion: Artificial jewelry interest + All tiers + Age 18-35
Behavior: Wedding planners, Gift buyers, Fashion bloggers

Q7: Creative kaun sa best hai - Image ya Video?

Video: 15-30 second, High engagement, Better storytelling
Image: Quick message, Lower cost, Easy to test
Best: Dono test karein, jo perform kare uspe budget daalein

Q8: Festival campaigns kab launch karein?

Start: Festival se 15-20 days pehle
Peak: Festival se 7-2 days pehle
Last minute: Festival se 1 day pehle (urgency ads)
Budget: Normal se 2x budget rakhein
